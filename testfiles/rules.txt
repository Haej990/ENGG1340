There are going to be 9 pieces of evidence and only 5 of them are valid to solve the crime.

The evidence will be randomly distributed in all 9 rooms on the map.

Collect all valid pieces of evidence and 1 key to open a secret room that the criminal is hiding.

Criminals have released a wild hound that can attack you, when it is nearby you have to type in "quiet".

The criminal also made traps so watch out.

Wrong spelling or getting into a trap will result in an empty bag.

You will have 3 chances, when it is zero, the game is over. 

Find the Criminal for us!
